The Challenge

The main challenge was the creation of a new feature for a widely known app. The main goals were for this feature to be something new that would add value for the users of the app.

To handle this challenge, we decided to develop a new feature for WhatsApp.

Why WhatsApp?

WhatsApp is the most popular mobile messaging app worldwide. WhatsApp has a major impact on the mobile phone industry and internet access in developing countries. WhatsApp focuses on encryption and privacy features and their impact on user security as one of their main goals.

Analysis and discovery

Did you know that an average user opens WhatsApp 23-25 times a day?

Such a number doesn't come as a surprise considering users spend 38 minutes using the app daily, which comes to about 19.4 hours a month, on average.

WhatsApp voice calls are also gaining steady popularity. On an average day, users spend a total of 2 billion minutes on WhatsApp voice and video calls.

Survey Analysis and Benchmark

Following the general analysis phase, we created a survey for WhatsApp users in order to find out what were the main pain points they felt and how they use the app on a daily basis.

We also asked what other apps they use, if they preferred them over WhatsApp, and why.
